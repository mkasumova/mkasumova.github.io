Requirements for Crocheting

  • A ball of yarn
  • A crochet hook
    • Crochet hooks are measured in millimeters. The bigger hooks go with the thicker yarn, and the smaller hooks go with thinner yarn.
  • Patience

Top 3 Things I Love About Crocheting

  1. Creative and thoughtful gifting has never been easier!
  2. A relaxing, fun hobby to do while watching TV, but also fun to do on it's own!
  3. If I want something, I can make it!
